What is legal design summit?

Legal Design Summit is the world's largest legal design event held in Helsinki on September 2023. Our mission is to create a forum for forward-thinking professionals to exchange knowledge and expertise on law, design and digital services through interdisciplinary collaboration.

Why attend?

Connect with international experts in the fields of law, design, and digital services.

Experience demonstrative presentations, interactive panels, creative case-studies, innovative talks, and so much more...
