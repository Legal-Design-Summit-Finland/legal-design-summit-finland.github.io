1 WHO IS THE CONTROLLER AND HOW I CAN CONTACT IT?

Legal Design Finland ry is the non-profit organization behind the event Legal Design Summit, BrainFactory and related side events. Our work towards making our events possible includes actions such as maintaining and providing the website legaldesignsummit.com as well as acquiring partners and speakers and providing related services such as online registration to our events.

To organize the event and to ensure the most wonderful experience for our participants, we collect and process personal data of Summit participants that have reserved a ticket through our ticket store, have sponsored the event through one of our partnership packages or have otherwise interacted with the Summit.

While doing this Legal Design Finland ry determines the purposes for which and the means by which personal data is processed, making Legal Design Finland ry the data controller.

The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to describe:

which personal data we process,

how and why the data is processed, and

what rights do you have as a data subject, and how you can exercise them to influence how your data is processed.

We at Legal Design Finland ry process your data responsibly and we want to be worthy of your trust. If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy – or if you for example want to exercise any of your rights as a data subject, you can always contact us using these contact details:

Processors are only allowed to process personal data for the purposes that we as the controller have defined.

We use Tito as our ticket management system and in this capacity. You can read their privacy policy here.
IS YOUR DATA TRANSFERRED BEYOND THE BORDERS OF THE EU OR EAA (“THIRD COUNTRIES”) Where Legal Design Finland ry is the data controller and uses data processors that are located outside of the EU/EEA, we do this only in cases where:
  • the transfers are either made to a country that is deemed to provide a sufficient level of privacy protection by the European Commission, or
  • the transfers are carried out by using appropriate legal safeguards such as standard data protection clauses (SCC) adopted, including any supplementary measures, where assessed to be necessary, or otherwise approved by the EU Commission or competent data protection authority in accordance with the GDPR.

4 HOW CAN YOU INFLUENCE THE PROCESSING OF YOUR PERSONAL DATA?

ou have many ways to influence how your data is processed by using your data protection rights provided by the GDPR.

If you wish to exercise any of the rights specified below or if you have any questions concerning your rights as a data subject, please contact us at privacy@legaldesignsummit.com.

RIGHT TO ACCESS

You have the right to access your personal data; meaning that you have the right to ask us to provide you with a report that includes what personal data we are processing related to you. Please note, however, that there are some exceptions to this rule: we can not, as an example, disclose your data that also reveals other people’s personal data.

RIGHT TO OBJECT

In general, you have the right to forbid the processing of your personal data when the legal basis is legitimate interest if exceptions do not apply.

RIGHT TO RECTIFY

You have the right to ask for rectification regarding your personal data: meaning that if something is not right in the data collected or we have gotten something wrong, you can always contact us and ask us to rectify the incorrect personal data.

RIGHT TO ERASURE

You have the right to the erasure of your personal data and to be forgotten; meaning that you can ask us anytime if you want to delete your personal data. There are, again, some exceptions to this rule: sometimes we have a legal obligation or other legal bases to keep some personal data even if you ask us to delete everything.

RIGHT TO DATA PORTABILITY

In certain situations, you have the right to receive the personal data provided to us in digital format so that you can transfer the data to a third-party controller.

RIGHT TO WITHDRAW CONSENT

If the processing of your personal data is based on consent given by you, you have the right to withdraw the consent at any time.

RIGHT TO RESTRICTION OF PROCESSING

In certain situations, you have the right to restrict the processing of your personal data for a period of time. For example, if you have chosen to use your rights to rectify or object and your requests are pending, you have the right to restrict the processing of your personal data for a period of time that allows us to ensure that your concerns are handled.

5 WHO CAN YOU TURN TO IF YOU HAVE QUESTIONS CONCERNING THE PROCESSING OF YOUR DATA?
In case you have any concerns or questions related to this Privacy Policy or processing of your personal data, you can always contact us via privacy@legaldesignsummit.com.
