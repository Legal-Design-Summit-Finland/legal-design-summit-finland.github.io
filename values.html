    Code of Conduct

    This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for all participants at Legal Design Summit (including volunteers, staff, attendees, speakers, and partners) and reflects the core values of Legal Design Finland ry. We are committed to creating a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for everyone, free from harassment and discrimination.

    By participating in the Legal Design Summit, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct.

    Our Values

    Legal Design Summit is committed to the following values:

    Selflessness

    We believe in supporting each other in achieving our shared goals. We see each other and the world with empathy, and never put personal gain before the interests of the community.

    Inclusion

    We create a space where everyone feels welcome and valued, and where diversity and different perspectives are celebrated. To support this, LDS is accessible to everyone through free tickets.

    Courage

    Learning, imagination and reaching for the impossible is in our backbone. We are curious about the world and passionate about improving it. When faced with a problem to solve, we ask: how hard can it be?

    Authenticity

    We are a movement dedicated to making a difference through creative design-thinking. We strive to make an impact by showing up as our authentic selves, while remaining honest, open and true to our core values.
