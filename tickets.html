FAQ

How much does it cost to attend?

Completely free! But there is a no-show fee of 50 (student tickets) -150â‚¬ (normal tickets)

Where can I get a ticket?

Link to the ticket release will be published on this site and our social media channels on Apr 10, 2023.

Can I get a ticket for 1 day only?

All tickets are for 2 days BUT you can share your ticket with someone else if you cannot participate on both days. However, the person acquiring the ticket is responsible for someone to show up on both days. Otherwise the general no-show fee applies.

Are there different types of tickets?

All tickets are essentially the same, including the same right to participate in the summit. There are, however, a limited number of student tickets which have a lower no-show fee. Student card must be presented at the entrance

Can I get a normal ticket even if I am a student?

Yes, you can, but please note then the no-show fee will be 150e!

How many tickets can I purchase?

Tickets are limited to one per person to increase inclusivity & accessibility of the summit

What if I need to cancel my ticket?

Tickets can be canceled until 14 August 2023 through the confirmation email you received from Ti.to

  • Click view order -> scroll down and you can find the 'view/edit ticket.'
  • Reassign/edit details OR scroll down to cancel your ticket. Cancelation cannot be undone
  • After that date, cancelation is no longer possible but you are free to find another participant yourself. Please note that you should inform us who will be participating with your ticket because the participant needs to show a valid ID when collecting your/their badge. Any chances or questions should be sent to: tickets@legaldesignsummit.com

Tickets are sold out, can I register for a waiting list?

Yes, you can register to the waiting list through the ticket release link. If a ticket comes available, you will get a registration link directly to your email. If you wish to secure a ticket, we have tickets set aside for out partners (both profit and non-profit). Please contact: partnerships@legaldesignsummit.com
