Legal Design Summit

11–13 SEP BRAINFACTORY | 14–15 SEP SUMMIT | HELSINKI, FINLAND



Legal Design Summit is a platform for forward-thinking professionals from all over the globe.

The Largest legal design event in the world takes place in the main hall of the University of Helsinki.

 

Today law lacks usability. Legal documents are poorly designed and inadequate to meet the needs of a modern society.

DESIGN THINKING WILL CHANGE THE PRACTICE OF LAW.
